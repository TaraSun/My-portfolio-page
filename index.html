About me

I am a Montana native currently residing in Williston, North Dakota with my significant other, Alec, and our German Shorthair Pointer, Dasher. I grew up in Billings, Montana and studied chemical engineering at Montana State University Bozeman where Alec and I met. After graduation, we picked up our lives and moved to Williston together for a job.

However, I was not finding a job that suited me, so I began to look into coding classes. This eventually introduced me to the web development world which was an immediate fit. I love having a job that allows me to work creatively and exercise my problem solving skills. I am currently working as a freelancer while taking more classes to improve my craft.
